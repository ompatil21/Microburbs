What the metrics mean (plain-English)

  • Price per sqm: price ÷ land size (sqm). Useful for comparing land value across listings.
  • Days on Market: days since listing_date. Shorter often implies stronger buyer demand.
  • Bedroom Mix: quick view of stock profile (e.g., more 3–4 bed houses).
